Unequal From Birth

A reporting project by the UC Berkeley Graduate School of Journalism

Policing

A focus of our project will center around policing, serving as watchdogs for communities that have little news coverage.

Child Welfare

Our reporters care deeply about child welfare. They are covering stories ranging from the child abuse epidemic, to the health crisis plaguing the Central Valley.

Community

Our project aims to better understand the Central Valley, and its geographic and cultural isolation from the rest of California.
